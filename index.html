Rubik's

I do the Rubik's cube!...I learned how to solve in 2007, but lost interest and didn't pick up a cube until Summer of 2013. From then on I was hooked! Cubes and methods:

  • 2x2: Dayan, CLL (working on EG-1). Average about 3.2
  • 3x3: Zhanchi/Guhong/Aolong 2, Roux. Average low 12's.
  • 4x4: Aosu, Yau. Average 1:25
  • 5x5: SS, Reduction. Average 3:00
  • Skewb: Moyu, Ranzha's method. Average 4.8.
  • Pyraminx: SS, Keyhole (working on one-flip). Average 6.2.
  • 3BLD: Guhong II, OP/M2. Average 1:45.

My favorite events are 3x3 speedsolve, skewb, 2x2 speedsolve, and 3x3 blindfolded.
