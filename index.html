Rubik's

I do the Rubik's cube!...I learned how to solve in 2007, but lost interest and didn't pick up a cube until Summer of 2013. From then on I was hooked! Cubes and methods:

  • 2x2: Dayan, CLL (working on EG-1). Average about 3.0
  • 3x3: Yuxin, Roux. Average 10.5.
  • 4x4: Aosu, Yau. Average 1:20.
  • 5x5: SS, Reduction. Average 2:30.
  • Skewb: Moyu, Ranzha's method advanced. Average 4.5.
  • Pyraminx: Moyu, Keyhole (working on advanced Keyhole). Average 6.2.
  • 3BLD: modded Guhong I, OP/M2. Average 1:20.

My favorite events are 3x3 speedsolve, skewb, 2x2 speedsolve, and 3x3 blindfolded.
